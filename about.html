About Us

Verdant Fork was founded by Temi Alabi, Sarah Lange, and Dami Omabuwa to promote sustainable, plant-based eating. Our mission is to provide healthy, eco-friendly meals while emphasizing inclusivity, minimalism, and global sustainability. Through thoughtful sourcing and water- and energy-efficient practices, we aim to inspire dietary wellness and environmental awareness across cultures. Grab a plate, and join us in building a healthier, more sustainable future!
